Cast the Dice to Find Your Fate

“The Chaunce of the Dyse” is a medieval poem that functions as a simplistic form of cleromancy, where each roll of three dice leads you to a specific stanza of the poem. Explore the manuscript text and discover your prognostication.

A digital critical edition of Bodleian Library MS Bodl. 638 and MS Fairfax 16.
