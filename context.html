Manuscript Context

This section will provide information on the two medieval manuscripts where the poem survives: Bodleian Library MS Bodl. 638 and Bodleian Library MS Fairfax 16.

Both manuscripts include digital facsimiles and show the unique marginal illustrations of dice rolls that accompany each stanza.
